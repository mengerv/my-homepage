Privacy Policy - Finn bomba!

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Finn bomba! does not collect, store, or share personal information from users.

What data the app uses

  • The app stores only required gameplay data locally on the device (for example settings and game progress).
  • This data is not transmitted to developer servers and is not shared with third parties.

In-app purchases

  • Purchases are handled by Apple (App Store / StoreKit).
  • We do not have access to payment details or Apple account credentials.

Children's privacy

  • The app is designed for families and does not collect personal data from children.
  • The app does not include third-party advertising or third-party analytics.
